Youth & Education

Education and youth engagement are central to the growth of the Muslim community in Rwanda. Islamic institutions, schools, and community programs focus on empowering young Muslims with knowledge, skills, and strong moral values.

Islamic Schools & Madrasas

Teaching the Qur’an, Arabic, and ethical principles while offering modern academic subjects to prepare youth for successful future careers.

Youth Programs & Leadership

Mentorship programs and leadership training at mosques and community centers that promote teamwork, critical thinking, and community service.

Educational Support

Providing scholarships, tutoring, and spiritual training programs to help students excel in both their academic and faith journeys.

Skills & Vocational Training

Teaching practical skills like computer literacy, entrepreneurship, and financial management to contribute to Rwanda’s broader development.

"Through these initiatives, young Muslims in Rwanda gain the knowledge, confidence, and leadership skills necessary to positively impact their families, communities, and the nation."
