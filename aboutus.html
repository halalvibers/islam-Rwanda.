ABOUT US

Welcome to Islam in Rwanda

Welcome to Islam in Rwanda, a website dedicated to sharing knowledge about the history, values, and contributions of Islam in Rwanda.

Our Mission: To educate people about Islam and highlight the presence and development of the Muslim community in Rwanda. Through this platform, we provide reliable information easily accessible to encourage learning, dialogue, and cultural understanding.

We also aim to promote understanding, unity, and respect among people of different religions and cultures. This website is created for students, researchers, and anyone interested in learning more about Islam and the Muslim community in Rwanda.

On this website, visitors can explore:

  • History of Islam in Rwanda
  • Influential Islamic Leaders
  • Mosques & Institutions
  • Learning Resources
  • Values & Principles
  • Ramadan & Eid Holidays
